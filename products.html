Solar PV Mounting Structures

Explore the most common solar mounting options and choose the right structure based on your site conditions, goals, and budget.

Rooftop Mounting Structure

  • Best for homes, apartments, schools, and factories with usable roof space.
  • Fastest installation with lower civil work requirements.
  • Supports net-metering and behind-the-meter savings.
  • Available in fixed tilt and elevated walkway-friendly designs.

Ground Mounted Structure

  • Ideal for open land and utility-scale projects.
  • Easy to scale from a few hundred kW to multi-megawatt plants.
  • Allows optimal row spacing for reduced shading losses.
  • Convenient access for cleaning, O&M, and periodic inspection.

Pole Mounted Structure

  • Space-efficient option for small land parcels and off-grid sites.
  • Elevated mounting improves airflow and panel cooling.
  • Can be designed for seasonal/manual tilt adjustment.
  • Useful where ground clearance is needed for farming or storage.

Tracking System Mounting Structure

  • Tracks sun movement to increase generation versus fixed-tilt systems.
  • Strong fit for high-radiation zones and utility-scale applications.
  • Delivers better land productivity and improved project IRR.
  • Available in single-axis and dual-axis configurations.

Carport Mounting Structure

  • Generates electricity while providing vehicle shade and protection.
  • Excellent for malls, hospitals, campuses, and commercial parking lots.
  • Improves site aesthetics and creates EV charging integration potential.
  • Modular layout enables phased expansion over time.

Elevated Solar Mounting Structure

  • Provides high ground clearance for dual land use.
  • Reduces flooding risk for electrical equipment in vulnerable areas.
  • Improves ventilation and module performance in hot climates.
  • Suitable for industrial yards, water-logged zones, and agro-solar projects.
