Solar PV Mounting Structures

Featured applications of our products for rooftop, ground-mounted, pole-mounted, tracking, carport, and elevated solar installations.

Rooftop Mounting Structure

  • Ideal for making use of existing roof areas without acquiring new land.
  • Suitable for residential, commercial, and industrial buildings.
  • Reduces electricity costs while supporting on-site energy independence.
  • Designed for safe installation, easy maintenance access, and long-term durability.

Ground Mounted Structure

  • Well-suited for open land and utility-scale solar projects.
  • Flexible row spacing and tilt for better sunlight capture.
  • Provides convenient access for cleaning, operation, and maintenance.
  • Scalable layout for expansion from captive plants to large solar parks.

Pole Mounted Structure

  • Space-efficient.
  • Elevated design.
  • Adjustable tilt & orientation.
  • Improved cooling & efficiency.
  • Off-grid capability.
  • Easy installation and maintenance.

Tracking System Mounting Structure

  • Increased energy generation.
  • Optimized sun exposure.
  • Improved system efficiency.
  • Cost-effective for utility-scale projects.
  • Real-time adjustment.
  • Land optimization.

Carport Mounting Structure

  • Dual functionality.
  • Efficient land use.
  • Vehicle protection.
  • Aesthetic and commercial value.
  • Cooler parking areas.
  • Revenue potential.
  • Scalable and modular.

Elevated Solar Mounting Structure

  • Better land utilization.
  • Improved cooling and efficiency.
  • Reduced shading.
  • Easier maintenance.
  • Flood safety.
  • Aesthetic and functional benefits.

Floating Solar Structure

  • Installed on lakes, ponds, and reservoirs where land is limited.
  • Improves panel efficiency due to lower operating temperatures.
  • Helps reduce water evaporation from exposed surfaces.
  • Excellent for industrial campuses and water utility projects.

Solar Pergola Structure

  • Combines clean energy generation with shaded public spaces.
  • Ideal for campuses, walkways, cafeterias, and recreational zones.
  • Supports premium architectural integration and branding.
  • Can be paired with EV charging or battery storage systems.

Agrivoltaic Structure

  • Designed to allow farming activity beneath elevated modules.
  • Creates dual income opportunities from crops and power.
  • Reduces heat stress on select crops in high-temperature zones.
  • Useful for rural feeders, farm co-operatives, and captive plants.
